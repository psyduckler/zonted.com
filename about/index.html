About

I'm Bernard Huang. I build things with AI.

I run tabiji.ai, a travel planning platform that uses Reddit data and AI to generate real itineraries. It's grown to thousands of pages, all built with AI agents.

Before that, I co-founded Clearscope, an SEO content optimization platform used by enterprise teams.

This site — Zonted — is where I document my experiments with AI tools. I test video generators, image models, music APIs, and content production pipelines, then write honest reviews and share what I learn.

I'm not a journalist or a reviewer by trade. I'm a builder who got tired of reading surface-level takes on AI tools. So I started testing them myself and publishing the results.
